1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide

We do not collect or store any personal information you input into the App, such as weight data or other health-related information. All data is stored locally on your device.

The App may request access to your camera to allow you to capture photos for personal tracking purposes. These photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared with us.

We use Google Analytics to track anonymized usage data, such as how often the App is used and which features are most popular. This data helps us improve the App’s functionality and user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics.
